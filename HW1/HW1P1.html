What is Kendama?

Kendama is an ancient skill toy that was created sometime in the sixteenth century. Today it is a global form of enjoyment that anybody can play and have fun with. There are large kendama tournaments all over the world. Professionals will come from all corners to comepete in these tournaments in hopes of winning and taking home cash prizes.
